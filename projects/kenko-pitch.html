KENKO_AAHARA

Nutrition Support for Chronic Illness Patients

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Kenko Aahara is a nutrition support platform designed for people living with chronic illnesses. We combine diagnosis-aware meal planning with ethical, culturally-sensitive delivery to help patients turn medical constraints into delicious, sustainable nutrition.

Current Phase: IDEATION & EARLY PLANNING

Progress: 35% Concept Definition

THE PROBLEM

Why This Matters

  • Chronic illness patients face overlapping dietary restrictions (medical, religious, cultural)
  • Existing meal planning tools ignore these constraints or treat them as afterthoughts
  • Current solutions lack cultural sensitivity and personalization
  • Patients often feel isolated in managing nutrition alongside their health condition

THE SOLUTION

A platform that bridges the gap between medical necessity and cultural/personal preference.

Core Features (Planned)

  • Diagnosis-aware meal recommendations based on medical conditions
  • Religion & cultural preference filters (halal, kosher, vegan, jain, etc.)
  • Allergy & ingredient tracking
  • Ethical sourcing and local delivery partnerships
  • Community support & meal sharing features

TARGET USERS

  • Patients with diabetes, hypertension, kidney disease, IBS, celiac disease
  • People requiring ethical/cultural meal accommodations
  • Caregivers managing nutrition for multiple family members
  • Underserved communities with limited localized meal options
